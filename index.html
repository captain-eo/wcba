Our Mission

To create a membership based network of businesses and individuals in White Center, Washington, designed to promote and protect the interests of member businesses and to uplift, unify and amplify the voices of our diverse community. This includes supporting new and existing businesses in the White Center business core and surrounding neighborhood, to advocate for and share resources that will help local businesses grow and thrive, and provide safe, fun spaces for all in our community.
